Crystal Collector
Click start to begin the game.

You will given a random number from 19 to 120.

As soon as you click start the number will disapper, don't forget it!

Each of the four crystal's will be assigned a hidden number. Click on the crystals to add your total guess which will start at 0.

Match your total guess to the random given number to win.

If you go over the random given amount you lose.
